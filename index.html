Who Am I? Why Programming?

Hi! My name is Cody Hogg. I am 23 years old and live in Sherwood, Arkansas. I have 3 children, a lovely wife, and 2 great dogs. My interest for programming began around the summer of 2022, after working 70-80 hour weeks, I decided I needed something new. I discovered programming from social media videos talking about how the job provided a great work/life balance, which is what I was needing. I learned programing from a variety of online courses, Codecademy, CS50, ZerotoMastery, mainly focusing my studies on Front-end development. As for a job, i'm open to any position. My main goal for my first job as a programmer is to find myself with a company who not only is well respected in the tech industry, but also is known for their teaching to their junior and intern developers. I am new to the programming world, however, I am a dedicated learner and spend a lot of time mastering the knowledge of whatever field I am in.
